Always loved programming - its like Lego without gravity.

Basic on my ZX81 graduating to assembler and Turbo Pascal during my teens.

Developed phone OS software - engineer, architect, product manager - but got made irrelevant by the iPhone and redundant by Android.

These days I mostly work with data, big data and fitting big data onto small boxes.

Competition: Mini Ludum Dare (21st-22nd Jan 2012) "Fear"

Prayers answered!  There’s a Ludum Dare game-making contest this weekend, and my wife happens to be going on a trip with the kids!  Its like the planets align, or something.

Sure its only a “mini” LD, a kind of practice, but I think that a practice before a first proper LD entry is a good thing!

I’ve created an account at LD - william_edwards - and I’ve got a ‘jam’ team together for this weekend.  Its the usual suspect, Mr War, as well as an excellent artist and modeler Adrian Delpha, also from the Glest modding community.
